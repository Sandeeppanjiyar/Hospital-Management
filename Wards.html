       In the GreenField Hospital Wards are
fully equipped with  modern  machinery to
observe  patient's  condition. There  are  4
types  of  Wards  in  the  Hospital:
(1) Non AC Male Ward
(2) AC Male Ward
(3) Non AC Female Ward
(4) AC Female Ward

       In the Wards, every patient is observed
time to time  by Doctors  and  Nurses. Here
Patient's  Meal  is  completely  free of  cost.
The Visiting Hours in all the Wards to meet
the patient is 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM.
