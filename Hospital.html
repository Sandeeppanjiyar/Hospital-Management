GreenField  Hospital  is  multidisciplinary
super-speciality  hospital. It  is  one of the
most prestigious hospitals of Kolkata was
established in 1999.

   The Hospital always  presents the latest
upgradations and  improvements to bring
world class standards to Kolkata not only
in medical technology  but also in patient
care and comfort.

   The Hospital is equipped with the finest
resources of medical science, its emphasis
is  on  relief,  reassurance,  recovery  and
rehabilitation.

   The indoor department has 250  beds in
the form of wards and cabins. 40 beds are
equipped  for  providing  Intensive  Care
Unit (ICU) and  25 beds are equipped  for
providing  Intensive  Cardiac  Care  Unit
(ICCU).

   Here  all  the  diagnostic  investigations
are carried out under the same roof. A few
to  name are MRI, CT Scan, Colonoscopy,
Dialysis,  ECG,  Ultra Sonography, etc.

   GreenField  Hospital  aims  to  provide
highest quality of service and cost-effective
care  and  treatment  to  each  and  every
patient, producing high level of satisfaction.
The  hosptial  trains  its  medical  and
paramedical staff time to time. The hospital
is committed to enhancing the quality of
life  through  improved  healthcare,  the
preparation of  those professionals  who
will serve the health needs of others and
discovery of knowledge that will  benefit
us all.
