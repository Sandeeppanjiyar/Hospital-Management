       GreenField Hospital offers  a  full  range
of  Intensive Care Unit  to manage  all  kinds
of critial  patients. There are  40 Beds present
in the ICU. The beds are equipped with  most
modern  equipments  &  gadgets. The  ICU is
equipped  with  bed-side  monitors  as well as
central  monitors to observe  all  the patients
from one point.

       Besides  Round  the  clock  Doctors  and
Nurses  are  present  in  the  ICU  to  observe
patients condition  time  to  time. There  is  a
separate ICU  for  Infectious Disease patients
where  separate  and  isolated  arrangements
are  present.
